## 2. App Description

AI Präsente is an iOS app for managing birthdays and gift ideas. The app provides the following features:

- Import of birthday data from iOS contacts
- Timeline view with filter options
- Management of gift ideas per person
- Reminder rules for birthdays
- Optional AI features for gift ideas and birthday messages
- iCloud synchronization between your devices
